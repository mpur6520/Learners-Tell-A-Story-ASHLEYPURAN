A Wonderful Story Featuring a Cat (and a girl, but the cat is more important)

It was a beautiful, sunny day when an unnamed cat roamed the busy city street.

He was very mischievous, he enjoyed knocking over tins and chasing people.

One day, the cat saw a small girl sitting alone, on a bench near the city park.

He could see rain--or maybe they were tears-- falling from where she sat.

The cat walked over to the girl, though not before hissing at a random person.

He jumped up on the bench next to her. The girl, startled, pays him no mind.

He jumps again, gaining her attention. She turns towards him, and the cat blinks.

'Oh, her eyes are raining,' he thinks. He doesn't care anymore, he wants a snack.

The young girl gives the cat a piece of salmon, out of nowhere. She's smiling now.

Salmon isn't his favorite. The cat will still take it. He nods in appreciation, and stares.

He purrs in dissatisfaction--if the weather is so easy to change, why does it rain?

The cat walks away from the girl, who's still smiling. He's off to kick some cans.
